Information about me and what I do.

Hi, I am a 28 year old Multimedia designer with a strong passion for digital storytelling, UI- and UX design and visual communication.
I combine creativity with technical skills to transform ideas into engaging digital experiences.
Since my childhood visual expression and design have been a natural part of my identity.
Throughout my Multimedia Design education at UCL have I expanded this passion into professional skills across multible platforms such as Figma, Adobe Lightroom, Photoshoop, After Effects, InDesign and Premiere Pro.
These tools allows me to work confidently within both interface design and digital content creation.
I continouously develop my skills through real world projects. I currently volunteer at a Social Drop In Center in Odense, where I contribute to their upcoming comedy revue through video production, graphic design, commercials and lighting concept.
Additionaly, I am starting up a teamwork with a school in Jytland redesing their website, and I collaborate with a start-up company on webdesign, persona development and SoMe strategy.
My goals as a designer is to grow inderpendently, build strong digital solutions, and explore new creative directions.
I value storytelling, atmosphere, visual identity and detail oriented design, elements that shape my work across UI, UX, motion graphic and branding.
Hiring me means welcoming a creative, curoius and dedicated designer who thrives in collaborative enviroments and work confidently across multible digital platforms.
I am eager to contribute with a fresh structured design thinking, and visually strong concepts.
